# Vi/Vim

* To UPPERCASE
  * `gUU`
* To case toggle
  * `g~~`
* To lowercase
  * `guu` or `guL`
  * 1 char `gul`

* search
  * `/keyword`
* no highlight
  * `:noh`

* change encoding
  * `:e ++enc=euc-kr`
  * `:set fileencoding=utf-8`
